What Are Those Sports El?

Basketball
Basketball is a dynamic sport played between two teams aiming to score points by shooting a ball into a hoop. It requires speed, agility, and teamwork. Invented by James Naismith, it has grown into a global phenomenon with leagues like the NBA and events such as the Olympics showcasing top talent.
Football
American football is a physical and strategic sport played between two teams aiming to advance the ball into the opponent’s end zone. It requires strength, speed, and teamwork. Popular in the United States, it features major leagues like the NFL and exciting events such as the Super Bowl.
Hockey
Hockey is a fast-paced sport played on ice, field, or indoors. It involves two teams using sticks to hit a puck or ball into the opponent’s goal. The game requires speed, skill, and teamwork. Popular worldwide, events like the Stanley Cup and Winter Olympics showcase top hockey talent.
Soccer
Soccer, also known as football, is the world’s most popular sport. Played by two teams of eleven, the game involves scoring goals by kicking a ball into the opponent’s net. It promotes teamwork, skill, and fitness. Soccer unites people globally through tournaments like the FIFA World Cup.
